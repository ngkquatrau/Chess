1.e4 e5 2.Ke2
The Bongcloud is a meme opening where White or Black move their king on the first few moves of the game. Among the worst openings in chess, the Bongcloud makes rare appearances on high-level games. However, high-profile players like GMs Hikaru Nakamura, Magnus Carlsen and Wesley So sometimes play it to lighten the mood of a match.
